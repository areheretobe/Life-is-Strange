Life is Strange

Life Is Strange is an episodic adventure game.

Max Caulfield, the protagonist, realize she has a power to turn back time after sees an illusion of storm.
Using the strange power, she tries to save her friend, Chloe Price.

The game is consisted of five episodes, beside Before the Storm, a prequel.
